## Executive Summary

München's lash studio market is split between two clear camps:
- **Quiet-luxury minimalists** (The Lash Club, Savoir Beauty) — premium branding, no prices displayed, strong visual identity
- **Service-breadth players** (Estetica Expert, Schönheitsrausch) — wide service menus, more utilitarian design

The common weakness across ALL competitors: **no transparent pricing**. This is a potential differentiator for studioofglamour.de. Online booking adoption is also mixed — only The Lash Club has a clear "Book Now" CTA with a dedicated booking page.

## What studioofglamour.de Should Do Better (Opportunities)

1. **Transparent pricing** — No competitor shows prices. Being the first to show a price list (even a "from X€" range) removes the biggest friction point for new clients and improves Google visibility.
2. **Online booking with a known platform** — The Lash Club has a "Book Now" CTA but an opaque backend. Using a visible platform (Booksy, Treatwell, or Calendly) with direct deep-link is a differentiator.
3. **Multilingual content** — DE + EN minimum. München has a large expat community; no competitor currently serves them well in English.
4. **Named artist / personal story** — The Lash Club's "Tessa" is the only competitor doing this. A personal face builds trust and differentiates from anonymous studio brands.
5. **Social proof on-page** — Instagram feed embedded, Google review count displayed, before/after gallery.

## Strategic Recommendations for studioofglamour.de

### Priority 1 — Immediate (Design/Content)
- Add a **price list page** — even approximate ranges. SEO and conversion impact is highest here.
- Add a **"Meet Your Artist"** section with photo and bio — copy The Lash Club's trust signal.
- Embed or prominently link a **booking system** with a brand-name platform (Booksy or Treatwell preferred for Munich discoverability).

### Priority 2 — Short Term (Differentiation)
- Launch an **English language version** (or at minimum English pricing/booking page) — zero competitors do this well.
- Create a **before/after gallery** page with proper alt-text for SEO.
- Add **Google Review count/stars** prominently in the hero section.

### Priority 3 — Medium Term (Market Position)
- Define a **single design concept** comparable to The Lash Club's "quiet luxury" or Savoir's "gold/green opulence" — a coherent brand identity that is instantly recognizable.
- Consider adding **Lashlift** as a lower-price-point entry service to capture clients who can't yet justify full extensions.
